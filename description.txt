创建角色的时候允许你直接为祂搭配时装
并且能保留自己的时装搭配！

在选择人物的界面多了样式复制按钮，在删除按钮左边
点击之后会复制这个人物的时装和外观，此时点新建就会直接应用

你可以在预设文件夹内为自己的预设样式重命名
也可以把你的时装搭配分享给你的朋友们。

Allowing you set vanities when create a character
And you can save these as presets!

In the menu of select characters, you can find copy button in the left of delete button
After click this, you will copy the vanity and style temple, now it will apply automatically when create a new character

You can rename the presets in the preset folder
You can share your works with your friends as well.
